K-Medi Sync - Step by Step Patient Guide

Patient Guidance Steps

Register as a New Patient

First, you need to fill out the patient registration form including your name, phone number, and password.

Login to Patient Account

After registration, scroll down to the "Patient Login" box and login using your registered phone number and password.

Select Doctor, Date & Time

Once logged in, select the available doctor, date, and time to book your appointment.

Check Appointment Status

After booking the appointment, go to the "Appointment Status" page and login again to check your status: Pending, Approved, or Rejected.

Appointment Confirmed!

If your status shows Approved, your appointment has been successfully confirmed.
